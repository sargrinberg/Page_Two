7 Weekends of Huzzah and Cheers!

Join King Henry and his royal court for an unforgettable journey back in time to his 16th-century European village, where adventure and excitement await at every corner. Explore 16 stages of captivating live entertainment, from daring jousts to theatrical performances, offering something for every member of the family. Wander through the vibrant Festival marketplace, where over 250 talented artisans showcase their handcrafted treasures, creating a truly unique shopping experience. Meet and interact with a host of colorful characters roaming the village streets, adding to the lively atmosphere. Celebrate 54 years of tradition and merriment at the Minnesota Renaissance Festival in Shakopee, MN. Huzzah!
